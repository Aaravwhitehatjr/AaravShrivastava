About #ExtinctPlastic

#ExtinctPlastic is a foundation where we stop plastic pollution by going to any place and cleaning up all the plastic which was inside the ocean. We made this foundation because we can save the world and we can recycle the old plastic by making it into useful products. As plastic pollution is the biggest issue faced in the world, all the animals are dying and it is said all of the oceans in the earth will be filled with plastic in 2050 and we do not want to make a miracle like this. So, with your contribution, we can save the world universe through your contribution.

To contribute and stop the rise of plastic pollution, you will have to go to the solution tab. After you click on the solution tab, there will be a ketto link. Ketto.org is a platform where you can make your own donation. After you click on the donate link, you can start contributing!
